# Bytify

The project mainly focus on building a **Assembly language simulator** with **MIPS** architecture.

## Function support in phase 1

1. Add
1. SUB
1. MUL
1. DIV
1. ADDI
1. SUBI
1. AND
1. OR
1. NOT
1. SLL
1. SRL
1. ANDI
1. LI
1. LA
1. MOVE
1. SLT
1. BNE
1. BEQ
1. Jump
1. Jump Break (Exit)
1. LW
1. SW
1. SYSCALL

### Programming Language used

`Python`
